# FetchMux weekly operating review

**Cadence:** once each week, 45 minutes  
**Owner:** operating lead  
**Evidence cutoff:** record date and timezone

The meeting is a decision system, not a progress performance. Unknowns remain unknown. Every number
must link to a real source, and every action needs one owner and one date.

## 5. Economics and capacity

```text
Beginning cash:
Cash in:
Cash out:
Ending cash:
Committed but unpaid obligations:

Founder hours already committed:
Hosted compute/log cost:

```

Calculate contribution only from actual customer receipts and actual attributable costs. Customer
provider usage is not FetchMux revenue or cost in the BYOK model.

Triggers:

- more than eight post-setup hours on one pilot: standardize, reprice, or stop;
- hosted infrastructure above 20% of subscription revenue at ten customers: redesign or reprice;
- unpaid custom work requested twice: add a priced scope or reject it;
- payment or tax setup incomplete: do not accept money.

## 7. Decisions

Every decision uses one of these verbs:

- **Continue:** evidence supports the current test.
- **Change:** state the evidence, changed variable, and next measurement date.
- **Stop:** close the experiment and remove ongoing cost.
- **Escalate:** external authority or specialist review is required.


## Day 21, 45, and 90 gates

- **Day 21:** three paid pilots after active approved outreach, or change segment/offer.
- **Day 45:** at least two qualified pilots show a meaningful metric or work reduction, or pivot to
  evaluation/observability or stop.
- **Day 90:** ten paying customers and repeatable delivery economics, or choose a better evidenced
  model rather than extending the deadline.
